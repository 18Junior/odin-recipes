Fried Chicken


I love fried chicken. Its taste never ceases to amaze me. It is easy to prepare and does not require alot of ingredients.

Ingredients needed are:

  • Flour
  • Cooking Oil
  • onion
  • pepper
  • buttermilk
  • eggs
  • salt

Steps used to prepare the chicken

  1. Coat the Chicken with Liquid(buttermilk or eggs)
  2. Dip the Chicken in the Flour Mixture
  3. Make sure the stove is on and the cooking oil is heated up
  4. Fry the Chicken for 12 to 15 minutes
